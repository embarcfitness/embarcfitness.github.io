Most virtual "races" are not competitive. First place goes to the runner with the easiest course.

Embarc's route generation technology allows race directors to have all of their participants run courses of similar difficulty, regardless of where the runners are located. This leads to a more enjoyable race day experience, and more sign-ups!

Let's Talk About Your Next Event

Let's improve your next race!

Embarc offers a one-of-a-kind virtual race. Here's how we do it:

  • Meet with your race team to discuss your event. No commitment required.

  • Determine the specifications (currently elevation gain and distance) of the race course. No matter where each runner is located, they'll run a course of similar difficulty to everyone else!

  • Create a page within the Embarc app specific to your event, featuring race leaderboards, technology support, and custom content you'd like to provide.

  • Allow your racers to instantly generate their race course from wherever they'd like to start their run.

  • Provide your team with a detailed "end of race report" to assist in recognizing top performing athletes.

Pricing

We charge per racer instead of a fixed fee, because offering a great virtual option should not mean risking your bottom line. For races of 50 athletes or less, we charge $6.99 per athlete. For races between 50 and 500 participating athletes, we charge $5.99 per athlete. For races with more than 500 athletes, contact us so that we can work something out!
